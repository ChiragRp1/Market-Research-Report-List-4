Radioisotope analysis involves the study of isotopes that emit radiation, such as those used in medical imaging and nuclear power. Stable isotope analysis, on the other hand, focuses on non-radioactive isotopes and is commonly used in fields like environmental science and archaeology. Both markets rely on isotope analyzers to accurately measure and analyze isotopic compositions for various applications and research purposes.</p></p>
