<p><p>Food starch market is categorized into four main types - Native Starch, Modified Starch, Starch Derivatives, and Starch Sweeteners. Native Starch refers to the unaltered form of starch derived from plants. Modified Starch undergoes physical or chemical modification to improve its properties such as thickening or gelling abilities. Starch Derivatives are obtained by further processing starch to produce products like glucose and dextrins. Starch Sweeteners are sugars derived from starch through enzymes or acid hydrolysis, used as sweetening agents in various food products.</p></p>
