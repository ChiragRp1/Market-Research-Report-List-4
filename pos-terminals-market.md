<p><p>A Point of Sale (POS) terminal is a device used by businesses to facilitate transactions with customers at the point of sale. The rise in e-commerce and digital payment methods has also contributed to the growth of the POS terminals market, as businesses seek to offer convenient and secure payment options to their customers.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the POS terminals market include the integration of advanced technologies such as NFC, EMV, and biometric authentication for enhanced security and efficiency. Mobile POS solutions are also gaining popularity, allowing businesses to accept payments on-the-go using smartphones and tablets.
