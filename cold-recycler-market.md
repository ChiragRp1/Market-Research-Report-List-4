<p><p>Cold Recycler Market Types refer to different technologies used for reclaiming and recycling asphalt pavement. Asphalt Emulsion Recyclers utilize an emulsion-based process to rejuvenate old asphalt materials, reducing the need for new resources. On the other hand, Foamed Asphalt Recyclers use a process that injects water and air into the asphalt mix to create a foam-like substance, improving its workability and sustainability. Both technologies are eco-friendly alternatives to traditional asphalt recycling methods.</p></p>
