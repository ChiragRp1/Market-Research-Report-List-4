<p><p>Abrasive tools can be classified into two main market types: natural and synthetic. Natural abrasive tools are made from materials found in nature such as sandstone, emery, and diamond. These materials are typically harder and more durable, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Synthetic abrasive tools, on the other hand, are man-made materials such as silicon carbide and aluminum oxide. These materials are designed to be more consistent in quality and performance, making them popular choices for precision grinding and polishing applications.</p></p>
