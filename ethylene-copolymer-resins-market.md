<p><p>Ethylene copolymer resins market can be classified into Type I and Type II based on their properties and applications. Type I resins have a higher density and are often used in applications where rigidity and impact strength are important, such as packaging materials and containers. On the other hand, Type II resins have a lower density and are commonly used in applications requiring flexibility and elasticity, such as films and coatings. Each type of resin caters to specific market needs and requirements.</p></p>
