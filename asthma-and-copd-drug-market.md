<p><p>The Asthma and COPD drug market can be categorized into four types: bronchodilators, anti-inflammatory drugs, monoclonal antibodies, and combination drugs. Bronchodilators work by relaxing the muscles in the airways, making breathing easier. Anti-inflammatory drugs reduce inflammation in the airways, helping to prevent asthma attacks. Monoclonal antibodies target specific proteins in the body that contribute to asthma and COPD symptoms. Combination drugs combine two different types of medications to provide more comprehensive treatment for asthma and COPD.</p></p>
