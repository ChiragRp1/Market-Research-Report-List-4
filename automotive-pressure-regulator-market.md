<p><p>Automotive pressure regulators help maintain a constant pressure level in the fuel system, ensuring optimal engine performance. Single stage regulators regulate pressure in one step, simplifying the design and providing cost-effective solutions. Double stage regulators, on the other hand, regulate pressure in two steps, ensuring precise control and increased safety by reducing the pressure in two stages. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining fuel pressure and ensuring smooth operation of the vehicle's engine.</p></p>
