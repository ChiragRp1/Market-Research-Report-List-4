<p><p>Thermal spray coating equipment market includes various types such as Plasma Spray, HVOF Spray, Arc Spray, and others. Plasma spray involves a high-temperature plasma flame to melt the coating material. HVOF spray uses a high-velocity spray of heated material to create a durable coating. Arc spray technique involves using an electric arc to melt the coating material. Other types of thermal spray coatings utilize different methods and materials to provide protection and improve the performance of various components in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and machinery.</p></p>
