<p><p>Bulldozer blades come in three main types: S-blade, U-blade, and S-U-blade. </p><p>S-blades are straight blades that are ideal for pushing materials straight ahead. They are commonly used for simple grading and leveling tasks. </p><p>U-blades have a curved shape that allows for more material to be carried while pushing. They are commonly used for large-scale earthmoving projects.</p><p>S-U-blades combine the features of both S-blades and U-blades, offering versatility in various applications. They are suitable for both pushing and carrying materials efficiently.</p></p>
