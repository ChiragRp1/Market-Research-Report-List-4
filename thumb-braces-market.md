<p><ul><li>Splint</li><li>Support Strap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thumb Braces can be divided into two main types: Splint and Support Strap. Splints are rigid braces designed to immobilize the thumb and provide maximum support for healing injuries or conditions. They are often used in cases of fractures or sprains. Support Straps, on the other hand, are flexible and provide compression and stability to the thumb joint without restricting movement. They are commonly used for conditions like arthritis or tendonitis. Both types offer different levels of support and are chosen based on the specific needs of the individual.</p></p>
