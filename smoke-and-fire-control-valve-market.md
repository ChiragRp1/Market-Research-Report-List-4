<p><p>Gravity-operated smoke and fire control valves rely on the natural force of gravity to close in the event of a fire, preventing the spread of smoke and flames. Spring-operated valves, on the other hand, utilize a spring mechanism to automatically close when triggered by heat or smoke, providing a quick response to fire emergencies. Both types of valves play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of buildings and occupants by containing fire and smoke within specified areas.</p></p>
