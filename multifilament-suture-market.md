<p><p>Multifilament suture market includes both absorbable and non-absorbable sutures. Absorbable sutures are designed to break down and be absorbed by the body over time, making them suitable for internal stitches where removal is difficult. Non-absorbable sutures, on the other hand, are made of materials like silk or nylon that do not break down, so they must be removed manually. Both types serve different purposes based on the needs of the patient and the type of surgery.</p></p>
