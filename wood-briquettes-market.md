<p><ul><li>Solid Cylindrical Type</li><li>RUF Type</li><li>Pini-Kay Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wood briquettes are available in three main types in the market - solid cylindrical type, RUF type, and Pini-Kay type. Solid cylindrical type briquettes are shaped like cylinders and are easy to handle, store, and use. RUF type briquettes have a rectangular shape with a hole in the middle, allowing for better airflow during burning. Pini-Kay type briquettes are dense and compact with a hole through the center, providing a longer burning time and higher heat output.</p></p>
