<p><p>Test tubes are essential laboratory equipment used for holding, mixing, or heating small quantities of liquid samples. They are available in two main market types: plastic and glass. Plastic test tubes are lightweight, cost-effective, and less prone to breakage, making them ideal for field and school use. On the other hand, glass test tubes are more durable, chemically resistant, and suitable for high-temperature applications in research labs and medical facilities. The choice between plastic and glass test tubes depends on the specific requirements of the experiment or analysis.</p></p>
