<p><p>Needle sensors are used in various industries for precise and accurate measurements. There are two main types of needle sensors - contactless sensors and piercing sensors. Contactless sensors use non-invasive methods to measure, without physically touching the object being measured. On the other hand, piercing sensors require the needle to physically penetrate the object to take measurements. Both types of sensors have their own advantages and applications, depending on the specific requirements of the industry.</p></p>
