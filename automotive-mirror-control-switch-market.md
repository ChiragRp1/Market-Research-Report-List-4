<p><p>The automotive mirror control switch market includes two main types: active optical switches and passive optical switches. Active optical switches use sensors to detect the driver's hand movements or facial gestures to control the mirrors electronically. Passive optical switches rely on manual input from the driver to adjust the mirrors. Active optical switches offer more advanced and hands-free control options, while passive optical switches require manual manipulation. Both types cater to different preferences and needs of drivers in the automotive industry.</p></p>
