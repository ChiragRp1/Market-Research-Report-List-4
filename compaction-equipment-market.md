These machines are powerful and efficient, capable of compacting thick layers of soil or asphalt. On the other hand, light compaction machines are more agile and versatile, commonly used for smaller projects like driveways or landscaping. They are ideal for compacting shallow layers of soil or asphalt with precision and ease.</p></p>
