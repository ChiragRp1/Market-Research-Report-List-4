Square port OMTs are commonly used in electronic warfare systems and satellite communication systems due to their broadband capabilities and high power handling capabilities. Circular port OMTs are typically used in radar and radio astronomy applications for their ability to handle circularly polarized signals with minimal interference.</p></p>
