<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cereal Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cross-flow</li><li>Down-stream</li><li>Mixed-flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cereal dryer market can be categorized into three main types: cross-flow, down-stream, and mixed-flow dryers. Cross-flow dryers have the hot air flow perpendicular to the grain flow, ensuring uniform drying. Down-stream dryers have the grain flow coinciding with the hot air flow, allowing for efficient and continuous drying process. Mixed-flow dryers combine elements of both cross-flow and down-stream dryers, offering a balance between uniformity and efficiency in drying cereal crops. These variations cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
