<p><p>Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitor (ABPM) market includes various types such as Ordinary ABPM and Mobile-based ABPM. Ordinary ABPM devices are typically used in clinical settings and require the patient to wear a cuff around their arm that inflates regularly to measure blood pressure over a 24-hour period. On the other hand, Mobile-based ABPM devices are more convenient as they can be worn throughout the day without interfering with daily activities, allowing for continuous monitoring of blood pressure levels.</p></p>
