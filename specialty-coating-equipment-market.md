PVD equipment involves the deposition of thin films on various surfaces, CVD equipment utilizes chemical reactions to deposit coatings, and thermal spray equipment applies coatings through a high-temperature spraying process. These different types of equipment cater to diverse industry needs for enhancing the performance and durability of products through specialized coating techniques.</p></p>
