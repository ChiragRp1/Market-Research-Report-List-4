<p><p>Enteral feeding bags are available in two main types: pump type and gravity type. Pump type bags are designed to be used with feeding pumps, which deliver a controlled amount of formula to the patient. These bags are often used in clinical settings or for individuals with more complex feeding needs. On the other hand, gravity type bags rely on gravity to deliver the formula steadily to the patient and are often used for more simple feeding regimens in home settings.</p></p>
