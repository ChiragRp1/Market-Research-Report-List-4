<p><ul><li>Powder XRD</li><li>Single-crystal XRD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-ray diffraction (XRD) instruments can be divided into two main market types: powder XRD and single-crystal XRD. Powder XRD instruments are used to analyze the crystal structure of powdered samples, making them ideal for identifying unknown materials or studying crystalline phases in a sample. Single-crystal XRD instruments, on the other hand, are designed for analyzing the atomic structure of single crystal samples, providing detailed information about the arrangement of atoms within the crystal lattice. Both types of instruments are essential tools in materials science, chemistry, and physics research.</p></p>
