<p><strong>The Shoulder Replacement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anatomical Shoulder Prosthesis</li><li>Reverse Shoulder Prosthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anatomical Shoulder Prosthesis is designed to mimic the natural anatomy of the shoulder joint, where the ball-shaped end of the humerus is replaced with a metal or plastic implant. This type of prosthesis is suitable for patients with intact rotator cuff muscles. Reverse Shoulder Prosthesis, on the other hand, is designed for patients with irreparable rotator cuff tears, where the ball and socket components of the joint are reversed. This unique design allows the deltoid muscle to compensate for the damaged rotator cuff, providing stability and improved function.</p></p>
