<p><p>Warehouse freight elevators are commonly categorized into two main types: Geared Traction Type and Gearless Traction Type. The Geared Traction Type elevator is equipped with a gearbox to control the speed and movement of the elevator, making it suitable for medium to high-rise buildings. On the other hand, the Gearless Traction Type elevator eliminates the need for a gearbox, offering a more efficient and reliable operation for taller buildings. Both types cater to different market demands based on building height and usage requirements.</p></p>
