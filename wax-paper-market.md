<p><p>Wax paper is available in two main market types: dry wax paper and wet wax paper. Dry wax paper is commonly used for food packaging, such as wrapping sandwiches and lining bakery trays. It is moisture-resistant and ideal for products that are not overly greasy or oily. Wet wax paper, on the other hand, is typically used for more heavy-duty packaging applications, such as wrapping meat or seafood.
