<p><p>SAW duplexer market is divided into two main types: unbalanced type and balanced type. Unbalanced type duplexer allows for easier integration into RF circuits due to its simpler design, but it has limitations in terms of performance. On the other hand, balanced type duplexer offers better isolation between transmitter and receiver signals, leading to improved overall system performance. However, it is more complex and typically more expensive to implement. Both types cater to different needs and requirements in various applications within the RF industry.</p></p>
