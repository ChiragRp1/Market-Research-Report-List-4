Digital Sensors convert the temperature reading into a digital signal that can be easily read by a microcontroller or other electronic device. Each type has its own advantages and applications, with Analog Sensors being more cost-effective and simple, while Digital Sensors offer greater accuracy and compatibility with modern electronic systems.</p></p>
