<p><p>Galvanized steel wire strands are categorized into 1X3, 1X7, and 1X19 based on their construction. 1X3 strands consist of three wires twisted together, providing flexibility and strength for various applications. 1X7 strands are composed of seven wires twisted together, offering even higher strength and durability. 1X19 strands are made up of nineteen wires twisted together, providing the highest strength and stiffness among the three types. Each type has its own unique characteristics and is used in different industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
