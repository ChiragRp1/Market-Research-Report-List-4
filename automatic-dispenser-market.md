<p><p>Automatic dispensers are categorized into two main types based on their placement: desktop and floor models. Desktop dispensers are designed to sit on a countertop or table, making them ideal for smaller spaces such as offices or kitchens. They are compact and easily accessible for users to dispense products like hand sanitizer or soap. On the other hand, floor dispensers are larger and typically stand on the floor, making them suitable for high-traffic areas such as lobbies or public restrooms. They have a higher capacity for product storage and often feature touchless technology for added convenience and hygiene.</p></p>
