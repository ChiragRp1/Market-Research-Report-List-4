<p><p>A Rubber Tyred Gantry Crane (RTG) is a mobile gantry crane used in intermodal operations to move and stack containers at ports and rail terminals. RTGs are equipped with rubber tires, which allow them to move around the terminal without the need for a dedicated rail track. These cranes are known for their high maneuverability, versatility, and efficiency in handling containers.</p><p>The Rubber Tyred Gantry Crane Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.3% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for efficient container handling solutions in ports and terminals worldwide. Additionally, the growing focus on sustainability and eco-friendly operations is driving the adoption of electric-powered RTGs to reduce environmental impact and operating costs in the long run.</p></p>
