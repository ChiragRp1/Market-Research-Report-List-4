<p><ul><li>Tamp Drum</li><li>Smooth Drum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-cylinder road rollers are primarily categorized into two types: Tamp Drum and Smooth Drum. Tamp Drum rollers have a padfoot drum with raised knobs or feet, designed for compacting cohesive soils like clay. On the other hand, Smooth Drum rollers have a smooth drum surface suitable for compacting granular materials like gravel or sand. Both types of road rollers are used for soil compaction in road construction, landscaping, and other infrastructure projects.</p></p>
