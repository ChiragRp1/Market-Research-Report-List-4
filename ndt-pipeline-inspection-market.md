<p><p>Automated Ultrasonic Testing, Magnetic Particle Testing, Radiographic Testing, Penetrant Testing, and Others are types of NDT Pipeline Inspection markets. Automated Ultrasonic Testing involves using automated systems to detect flaws in pipelines. Magnetic Particle Testing uses magnetic fields to identify surface and near-surface flaws. Radiographic Testing uses X-rays or gamma rays to inspect internal structures. Penetrant Testing involves applying a liquid dye penetrant to detect surface-breaking flaws. The Others category includes various additional NDT techniques used for pipeline inspection.</p></p>
