<p><strong>The Quartz Oscillators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TCXO</li><li>VCXO</li><li>OCXO</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quartz oscillators are classified into various types based on their specific functionalities and applications. TCXO (Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillators) are known for their stable frequency output over a wide temperature range. VCXO (voltage-controlled crystal oscillators) can be tuned by varying the input voltage, making them suitable for frequency modulation. OCXO (oven-controlled crystal oscillators) use a temperature-controlled oven to maintain precise frequency stability. Other types of quartz oscillators include MEMS-based oscillators and SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) oscillators. Each type caters to different requirements in various industries.</p></p>
