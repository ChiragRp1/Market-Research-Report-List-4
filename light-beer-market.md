<p><ul><li>Limit Fermentation</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light beer market types can include limiting fermentation to reduce alcohol content or utilizing dealcoholization methods. Limiting fermentation involves stopping the brewing process before all sugars are converted into alcohol, resulting in a lower alcohol content. Dealcoholization methods involve removing alcohol from already brewed beer through techniques such as vacuum distillation or reverse osmosis. Both of these methods cater to consumers looking for lighter beer options with reduced alcohol content while still maintaining flavor and quality.</p></p>
