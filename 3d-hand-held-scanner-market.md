<p><p>The 3D hand-held scanner market includes two main types: laser scanners and structured light scanners. Laser scanners use laser beams to scan and capture 3D objects, offering high accuracy and precision. Structured light scanners project patterns of light onto objects and use cameras to capture the deformations of these patterns, providing detailed surface information. Both technologies are widely used in various industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and entertainment for applications like quality control, reverse engineering, and animation.</p></p>
