<p><strong>The Corrugating Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet End Equipment</li><li>Dry End Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrugating machinery market is segmented into Wet End Equipment and Dry End Equipment. Wet End Equipment refers to machines used in the initial stages of the corrugating process, such as corrugating rolls and splicers. Dry End Equipment, on the other hand, includes machines used in the final stages of the process, such as slitter scorers and stackers. Both types of equipment are essential in the production of corrugated materials, with Wet End Equipment forming the corrugated board and Dry End Equipment finishing the product for packaging purposes.</p></p>
