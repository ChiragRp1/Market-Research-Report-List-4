<p><p>PV Tracker Systems are used to maximize energy production in solar power plants by following the movement of the sun. Single Axis Trackers rotate on one axis to adjust the angle of the solar panels throughout the day, increasing energy output. Dual Axis Trackers adjust on both horizontal and vertical axes, further optimizing panel orientation for maximum sunlight exposure. Single Axis Trackers are more common due to their cost-effectiveness, while Dual Axis Trackers provide even higher energy efficiency but come with a higher price tag.</p></p>
