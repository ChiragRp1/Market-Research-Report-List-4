<p><p>Duroscope Market Types include Brinell, Vickers, Rockwell, and others. Brinell Duroscope measures hardness by indenting a specified load into the material surface. Vickers Duroscope also measures hardness through indentation using a pyramid-shaped diamond. Rockwell Duroscope measures hardness by applying a load onto the material surface and then measuring the depth of penetration. Other types of Duroscopes may use different methods to determine material hardness. Each type of Duroscope is used in various industries to assess the durability and strength of different materials.</p></p>
