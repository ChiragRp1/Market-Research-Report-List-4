<p><p>Semiconductor test boards for the military market come in various types, including probe cards, load boards, and burn-in boards (BIBs). Probe cards are used to make contact with the device under test, load boards provide power and signals to the device, while burn-in boards are used for testing the reliability and durability of the device over an extended period of time. Each type of test board plays a critical role in ensuring the quality and performance of semiconductor components used in military applications.</p></p>
