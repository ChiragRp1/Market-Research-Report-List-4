<p><p>Moulding Starch market can be categorized into three types based on their digestibility: Rapidly Digestible Starch, Slowly Digestible Starch, and Resistant Starch. Rapidly Digestible Starch is quickly broken down by the body for immediate energy. Slowly Digestible Starch provides a sustained release of energy over time. Resistant Starch resists digestion in the small intestine and acts as a prebiotic, promoting gut health. Each type of starch offers different benefits and applications in the food industry.</p></p>
