<p><strong>The Disc Brake Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston Type</li><li>Floating Caliper Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disc Brake Systems are divided into two main types: Piston Type and Floating Caliper Type. </p><p>Piston Type brake systems use multiple pistons within the caliper to create pressure against the brake pads, allowing for better control and stopping power.</p><p>Floating Caliper Type brake systems use a single piston that moves within the caliper, allowing for simpler design and lighter weight. However, they may provide slightly less control and stopping power compared to Piston Type systems. Both types are commonly found in the automotive industry.</p></p>
