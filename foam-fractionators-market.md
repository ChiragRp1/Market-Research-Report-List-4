<p><p>Foam fractionators are used in aquariums to remove organic compounds and dissolved proteins that cause foaming. Internal foam fractionators are installed inside the aquarium filtration system, while hang-on foam fractionators are mounted externally. Sump based foam fractionators are placed in the sump of the aquarium system. These different types of foam fractionators cater to varying tank sizes and filtration needs, providing an effective solution for maintaining water quality and clarity in aquarium environments.</p></p>
