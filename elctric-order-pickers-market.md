<p><p>Electric order pickers are classified into three types based on the lift height capabilities: low level, medium level, and high level. Low level order pickers are designed for lower lift heights and are ideal for basic picking tasks. Medium level order pickers can reach moderate heights, making them suitable for warehouses with taller racks. High level order pickers have the highest lift height capabilities, allowing for efficient picking in warehouses with extremely high racks. Each type caters to different warehouse needs depending on the lift height requirements.</p></p>
