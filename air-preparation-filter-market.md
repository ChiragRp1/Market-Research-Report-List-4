<p><strong>The Air Preparation Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Filtering</li><li>Chemisorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air preparation filters are used to remove impurities from compressed air before it reaches pneumatic equipment. Physical filtering involves the use of porous materials to trap particles such as dust and oil, while chemisorption filters use chemicals to remove gases and odors from the air. Physical filtering is more commonly used for general air purification, while chemisorption is often employed in industrial settings where specific contaminants need to be removed. Both types of filters play a crucial role in maintaining the quality of compressed air in various applications.</p></p>
