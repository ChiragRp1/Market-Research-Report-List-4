<p><p>Split air conditioning systems are available in various types to suit different needs. Mono-Split systems consist of one indoor unit connected to one outdoor unit. Multi-Split systems have multiple indoor units connected to one outdoor unit. Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) systems use one outdoor unit to control multiple indoor units simultaneously. Floor Ceiling systems can be installed either on the floor or ceiling, providing flexibility in placement. Each type offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
