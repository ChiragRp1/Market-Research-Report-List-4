<p><ul><li>White Pepper Powder</li><li>Black Pepper Powder</li><li>Green Prickley Ash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pepper powder typically comes in three main types - white pepper powder, black pepper powder, and green prickly ash powder. White pepper powder is made from ripe peppercorns with the outer skin removed, giving it a milder flavor. Black pepper powder is made from unripe peppercorns that are dried and ground, providing a stronger and spicier taste. Green prickly ash powder, on the other hand, is made from the prickly ash tree and is known for its numbing and tingling sensation on the tongue.</p></p>
