<p><p>Male Hypogonadism market includes two main types of therapies: Testosterone Replacement Therapy and Gonadotropin-Releasing Hormones Therapy. Testosterone Replacement Therapy involves providing testosterone to individuals with low levels of the hormone, either through injections, patches, gels, or implants. Gonadotropin-Releasing Hormones Therapy works by stimulating the production of testosterone through the use of medications that mimic the action of gonadotropin-releasing hormones. Both therapies aim to alleviate symptoms of male hypogonadism, such as low libido, fatigue, and infertility.</p></p>
