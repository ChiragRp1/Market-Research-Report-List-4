<p><strong>The Anaesthesia Vaporizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plenum Vaporizers</li><li>Drawover Vaporizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anaesthesia vaporizers are devices used to administer volatile anaesthetic agents to patients during surgery. Plenum vaporizers are fixed to the anaesthesia machine and deliver a precise concentration of anaesthetic agent using a series of chambers and valves. Drawover vaporizers are portable devices that do not require electricity, relying on the patient's inhalation to draw anaesthetic agent from the vaporizer. Both types of vaporizers are crucial in providing safe and effective anaesthesia during medical procedures.</p></p>
