<p><p>Digital absolute pressure gauges come in two main types: fixed probe and connectable probe. Fixed probe gauges have a built-in probe that cannot be removed or changed, offering a more compact and integrated solution. On the other hand, connectable probe gauges feature a detachable probe that can be connected to different devices or systems for versatile applications. Each type has its own advantages, with fixed probe gauges being more user-friendly and connectable probe gauges offering flexibility and adaptability.</p></p>
