<p><ul><li>Linear Motion</li><li>Rotary Motion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric actuators for aircraft can be divided into two main types: linear motion and rotary motion. </p><p>Linear motion actuators are used to convert electrical energy into linear mechanical force, allowing for precise control of movements in a straight line. These actuators are commonly used in functions such as landing gear extension and retraction.</p><p>Rotary motion actuators, on the other hand, convert electrical energy into rotational mechanical force, enabling control of movements in a circular motion. These actuators are essential for functions such as controlling flaps and rudders on aircraft.</p></p>
