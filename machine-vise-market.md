<p><ul><li>Single Station</li><li>Dual Station</li><li>Multiple Station</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Machine vise market is categorized into three types based on the number of workstations they have. Single station vises are designed for holding one workpiece at a time, offering precision and stability. Dual station vises allow for two workpieces to be held simultaneously, increasing efficiency in production processes. Multiple station vises are equipped with three or more workstations, enabling multiple parts to be machined at once, enhancing productivity. Each type of vise caters to different needs and requirements of the machining industry.</p></p>
