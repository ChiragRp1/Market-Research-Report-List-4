<p><ul><li>Static Torque Sensor</li><li>Dynamic Torque Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static Torque Sensors are used to measure the torque of a stationary object or system, providing precise and accurate measurements in a fixed position. These sensors are commonly used in applications where the torque does not change rapidly or continuously.</p><p>Dynamic Torque Sensors, on the other hand, are designed to measure torque in systems that are in motion or experiencing dynamic changes in torque. These sensors are ideal for applications where the torque levels fluctuate rapidly and require real-time monitoring and analysis. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries, providing essential data for performance optimization and quality control.</p></p>
