<p><strong>The Smartphone Camera Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-illuminated Sensor</li><li>Backside-illuminated Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smartphone camera sensor market includes two main types: Front-illuminated sensor and backside-illuminated sensor. Front-illuminated sensors have a layer of wiring on the light-sensitive side, which can block some incoming light and result in lower image quality. On the other hand, backside-illuminated sensors have wiring on the backside, allowing more light to reach the sensor and produce higher quality images. Manufacturers choose between these options based on factors such as cost, performance, and desired image quality.</p></p>
