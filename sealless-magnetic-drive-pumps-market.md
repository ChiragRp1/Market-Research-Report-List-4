<p><p>Sealless magnetic drive pumps can be categorized into two main types based on the shaft configuration: stationary shaft and rotating shaft. In stationary shaft pumps, the impeller is directly attached to the motor shaft, eliminating the need for a dynamic seal. On the other hand, rotating shaft pumps have a separate pump shaft that transmits torque from the motor to the impeller. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different industries and operating conditions.</p></p>
