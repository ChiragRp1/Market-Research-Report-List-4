<p><p>Ship exhaust gas scrubbers are systems used to reduce harmful emissions from ships' engines. There are three main types of scrubbers on the market: open loop, closed loop, and hybrid scrubbers. Open loop scrubbers use seawater to remove pollutants from the exhaust gases. Closed loop scrubbers use a circulating solution to clean the emissions, while hybrid scrubbers can switch between open and closed loop modes depending on the operating conditions. Each type offers advantages and disadvantages in terms of cost, efficiency, and environmental impact.</p></p>
