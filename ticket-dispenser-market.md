<p><p>Ticket dispensers can be categorized into two main types: manual and automatic. Manual ticket dispensers require users to physically take a ticket from the machine, while automatic ticket dispensers distribute tickets without the need for user intervention. Manual ticket dispensers are more commonly found in small businesses or events where the volume of customers is manageable. On the other hand, automatic ticket dispensers are typically used in locations with high customer traffic such as airports, train stations, or amusement parks.</p></p>
