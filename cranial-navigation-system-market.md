They are known for their high accuracy and reliability.</p><p>On the other hand, electromagnetic systems utilize electromagnetic fields to track instruments within the body. They offer real-time tracking without line-of-sight constraints, making them suitable for complex procedures.</p><p>Both types of systems play a crucial role in enhancing accuracy and precision in cranial surgeries.</p></p>
