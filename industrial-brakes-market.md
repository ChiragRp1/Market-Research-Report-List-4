<p><p>The industrial brakes market can be categorized into three main types: disc brakes, drum brakes, and others. Disc brakes provide efficient braking performance due to their direct contact with the brake pads, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Drum brakes work by applying friction from internally expanding brake shoes, making them ideal for continuous, high-speed operations. The "others" category may include brake systems such as electromagnetic brakes or hydraulic brakes, offering unique features and benefits for specific industrial applications.</p></p>
