<p><p>Mycoplasma diagnostics devices market includes various types such as PCR (polymerase chain reaction), immunoassay, DNA staining, microbial culture techniques, and other methods. PCR is a molecular technique that amplifies DNA to detect the presence of Mycoplasma. Immunoassays detect antibodies or antigens in the body's immune response to the bacteria. DNA staining involves labeling Mycoplasma DNA with a fluorescent dye for visualization. Microbial culture techniques involve growing Mycoplasma in a laboratory setting. Other methods may include molecular techniques such as sequencing or hybridization.</p></p>
