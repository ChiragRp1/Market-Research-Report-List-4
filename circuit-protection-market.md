Overcurrent protection safeguards electronic circuits from excess current flow, preventing damage to components. ESD protection shields against electrostatic discharge, which can harm sensitive electronics. Overvoltage protection defends circuits from voltage spikes that could cause malfunctions or failures. These types of protection are essential for maintaining the reliability and longevity of electronic devices, ensuring they operate safely and efficiently.</p></p>
