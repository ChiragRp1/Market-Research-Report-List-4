<p><p>Industrial centrifuge machines are segmented into different types based on their operation and design. Sedimentation centrifuges separate solid particles from liquids by gravitational force. Filtering centrifuges use a filter media to separate solids from liquids. Basket centrifuges utilize a rotating basket to separate materials of different densities. Scroll screen centrifuges use a rotating screen to separate solids from liquids. Peeler centrifuges peel off solids from liquids using a scraper. Pusher centrifuges push solids out of the machine. There are also other types of industrial centrifuge machines available in the market.</p></p>
