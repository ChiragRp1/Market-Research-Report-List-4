<p><ul><li>Pulsed Fibre Laser</li><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fibre Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fibre laser machines can be categorized into two main types: Pulsed fibre laser and Continuous Wave (CW) fibre laser. Pulsed fibre lasers generate high-intensity laser pulses in short bursts, making them suitable for applications requiring high peak power. On the other hand, CW fibre lasers emit a continuous beam of laser light, allowing for steady and consistent laser processing. Both types of fibre lasers have their unique advantages and applications, making them popular choices in the industrial laser market.</p></p>
