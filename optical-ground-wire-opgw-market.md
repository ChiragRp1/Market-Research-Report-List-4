<p><strong>The Optical Ground Wire (OPGW) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPGW</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPGW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Ground Wire (OPGW) market includes two main types: Central Tube Structure OPGW and Layer Stranding Structure OPGW. Central Tube Structure OPGW consists of optical fibers enclosed within a central tube, providing high tensile strength and resistance to bending. On the other hand, Layer Stranding Structure OPGW has optical fibers laid in layers around a stainless steel tube, offering flexibility and easier installation. Both types are commonly used in overhead power line installations for communication and monitoring purposes.</p></p>
