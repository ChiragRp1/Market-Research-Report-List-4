<p><ul><li>Level Death Benefit</li><li>Modified or Graded Death Benefit</li><li>Guaranteed Acceptance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Burial insurance typically falls into three market types: Level Death Benefit, Modified or Graded Death Benefit, and Guaranteed Acceptance. Level Death Benefit policies offer full coverage from day one, while Modified or Graded Death Benefit policies provide partial coverage initially that increases over time. Guaranteed Acceptance policies do not require a medical exam or health questions, making them ideal for individuals with pre-existing conditions. Each market type has its own unique features, providing options for individuals with different needs and circumstances.</p></p>
