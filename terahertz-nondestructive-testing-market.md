<p><p>Terahertz Nondestructive Testing (NDT) market includes various types of testing methods such as Eddy Current Testing, Ultrasonic Testing, Radiography, Sonic Testing, Magnetic Particle Testing, and others. Eddy Current Testing employs electromagnetic induction to detect flaws in conductive materials. Ultrasonic Testing uses high-frequency sound waves to detect internal defects. Radiography uses X-rays or gamma rays to inspect the internal structure of an object. Sonic Testing uses sound waves to evaluate the integrity of materials. Magnetic Particle Testing identifies flaws in ferromagnetic materials. Other methods include dye penetrant testing and visual inspection.</p></p>
