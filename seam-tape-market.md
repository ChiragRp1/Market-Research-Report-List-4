On the other hand, multi-layered seam tape is composed of multiple layers of materials for added strength and durability, making it suitable for heavy-duty fabrics or more complex seam sealing tasks. Both types of seam tape offer effective waterproofing and bonding properties, but their usage may vary depending on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
