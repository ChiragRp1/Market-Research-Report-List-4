Single Zone systems have one detection zone for identifying abnormal rhythms, while Dual Zone systems have two detection zones for more precise detection and therapy delivery. Dual Zone systems offer greater customization and flexibility in treating different types of heart rhythm abnormalities compared to Single Zone systems.</p></p>
