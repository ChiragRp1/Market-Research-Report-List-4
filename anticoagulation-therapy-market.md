<p><p>Anticoagulation therapy market includes two main types: NOACs (novel oral anticoagulants) and Warfarin. NOACs are newer medications that work by targeting specific clotting factors in the blood, while Warfarin is a traditional anticoagulant that has been used for decades. NOACs are considered more convenient for patients as they require less monitoring and have fewer drug interactions, while Warfarin requires regular blood testing and dietary restrictions. Both types of medications are commonly used to prevent blood clots in patients with conditions such as atrial fibrillation or deep vein thrombosis.</p></p>
