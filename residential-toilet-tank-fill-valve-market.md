<p><p>The residential toilet tank fill valve market is mainly categorized into two types: Float Ball and Float Cup. Float Ball fill valves operate using a buoyant ball attached to an arm that rises and falls with the water level. Float Cup fill valves, on the other hand, use a cylindrical float cup to regulate the water level. Both types of fill valves serve the same purpose of controlling the flow of water into the toilet tank to maintain the desired water level.</p></p>
