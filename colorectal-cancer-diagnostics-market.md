<p><p>Colorectal cancer diagnostics market includes various types of tests such as FOB tests, CTC tests, CEA tests, and others. FOB tests detect blood in the stool, indicating the presence of cancer. CTC tests detect circulating tumor cells in the blood, while CEA tests measure levels of a protein that may be elevated in colorectal cancer. Other tests include genetic, molecular, and imaging tests used for diagnosis and screening of colorectal cancer. Each test plays a crucial role in the early detection and management of the disease.</p></p>
