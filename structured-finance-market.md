<p><p>Structured finance market types include Assets Backed Securities (ABS), Collateralized Debt Obligations (CDO), and Mortgage-Backed Securities (MBS). ABS are securities backed by a pool of assets such as credit card receivables. CDOs are securities backed by a diversified pool of debt obligations. MBS are securities backed by a pool of mortgages. These types of structured finance allow for the securitization and trading of various types of assets, providing investors with opportunities for diversification and risk management.</p></p>
