<p><p>Geomembranes Market Types include Bituminous Geomembranes and Thermoplastic Geomembranes. Bituminous Geomembranes are composed of bitumen and additives, providing high tensile strength and puncture resistance. They are commonly used in landfill liners and construction projects. Thermoplastic Geomembranes, on the other hand, are made from various polymers such as HDPE, LDPE, and PVC. They offer excellent chemical resistance and flexibility, making them ideal for applications in environmental protection, agriculture, and mining industries. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring environmental protection and containment solutions.</p></p>
