<p><p>Stationary POS scanners are classified into three main types based on their design and functionality. Presentation scanners are handheld scanners that are used to scan large or bulky items quickly and efficiently. Bioptic scanners are designed for high-speed scanning in high-traffic environments such as supermarkets. Mini-slot scanners are compact scanners that are ideal for scanning small items or items with irregular shapes. Each of these types of scanners is designed to meet specific needs in the retail industry and improve the overall efficiency of the checkout process.</p></p>
