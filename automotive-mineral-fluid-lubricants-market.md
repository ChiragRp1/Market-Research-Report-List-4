<p><strong>The Automotive Mineral Fluid Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paraffinic Lubricant</li><li>Naphtenic Lubricant</li><li>Aromatic Lubricant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive mineral fluid lubricants come in three main types based on the hydrocarbon structure: paraffinic, naphthenic, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants are characterized by straight-chain hydrocarbons, providing good oxidative stability and low pour points. Naphthenic lubricants contain cycloalkanes, offering good solvency and low temperature properties. Aromatic lubricants have a higher degree of unsaturation, providing good chemical stability and resistance to oxidation. Each type of lubricant has specific properties that make them suitable for different automotive applications.</p></p>
