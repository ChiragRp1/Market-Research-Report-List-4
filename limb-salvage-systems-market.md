<p><p>Limb Salvage Systems Market includes three main types of products: Allo-metal prostheses, Metal prostheses, and Allografts. Allo-metal prostheses combine both biological and metal components to preserve limb function. Metal prostheses are fully metallic implants used to replace bone or joint defects. Allografts involve the transplantation of bone or tissue from a donor, usually cadaveric, to reconstruct damaged limbs. These systems are designed to provide effective alternatives to amputation and improve quality of life for patients with limb-threatening conditions.</p></p>
