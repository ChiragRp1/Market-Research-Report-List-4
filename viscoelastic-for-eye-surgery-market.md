Two common types of viscoelastics used in these procedures are Sodium Hyaluronate and Hydroxypropyl Methyl Cellulose. Sodium Hyaluronate is a naturally occurring substance in the body that provides lubrication and cushioning. Hydroxypropyl Methyl Cellulose is a synthetic polymer that forms a protective barrier on the eye's surface. Both materials help to improve surgical outcomes and patient comfort during eye surgeries.</p></p>
