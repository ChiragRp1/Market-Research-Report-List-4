<p><p>Cryostat Market is segmented into various types including Closed-Cycle, Continuous-Flow, Bath, and Multistage Cryostats. Closed-Cycle Cryostats operate with a closed refrigeration system, providing stable and precise temperature control. Continuous-Flow Cryostats allow continuous cooling of samples using flowing cryogens. Bath Cryostats utilize a cryogenic liquid bath for cooling samples. Multistage Cryostats consist of multiple cooling stages to achieve extremely low temperatures. These different types cater to a wide range of applications in various industries such as healthcare, research, and semiconductor.</p></p>
