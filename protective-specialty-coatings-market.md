<p><strong>The Protective & Specialty Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne Coatings</li><li>Water-borne Coatings</li><li>Powder Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Protective & Specialty Coatings market comprises three main types: Solvent-borne Coatings, Water-borne Coatings, and Powder Coatings. Solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents as a carrier, offering excellent durability and resistance, but have environmental concerns. Water-borne coatings are environmentally friendly, with low VOC content, but may require more drying time. Powder coatings are known for their durability, resistance, and environmental friendliness, as they contain no volatile organic compounds and overspray can be recycled. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in the protective coatings market.</p></p>
