<p><p>The Sinusoidal Module Oscillator market can be divided into two main types based on the nature of the oscillations: Damped and Undamped. </p><p>Damped oscillation refers to the type of oscillation where the amplitude of the oscillations decreases over time due to the presence of external forces or factors that dampen the oscillations.</p><p>Undamped oscillation, on the other hand, refers to the type of oscillation where there are no external forces or factors causing dampening, resulting in the oscillations maintaining a constant amplitude over time.</p></p>
