<p><p>DUV Lithography Systems are used in the semiconductor industry for high-resolution patterning of integrated circuits. ArF Dry Excimer Systems use a 193nm wavelength laser to achieve precise patterning. ArF Immersion Excimer Systems utilize a 193nm laser with a liquid immersion process for even higher resolution. KrF Excimer Systems operate at a longer wavelength of 248nm, offering an alternative for less demanding applications. Each type of system caters to different requirements in the semiconductor manufacturing process, offering varying levels of resolution and performance.</p></p>
