<p><p>Automatic weigh labellers are commonly categorized into two types: semi-automatic and fully automatic. </p><p>Semi-automatic weigh labellers require some manual intervention, such as placing the product on the scale, before printing and applying the label. This makes them suitable for smaller scale operations with lower volume production.</p><p>On the other hand, fully automatic weigh labellers are capable of weighing, printing, and applying labels without the need for manual input. They are ideal for high volume production lines where efficiency and accuracy are key.</p></p>
