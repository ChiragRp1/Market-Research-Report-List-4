<p><p>Fence/Wall Mounted Pool Alarm systems are installed on the perimeter of the pool area and are triggered when someone tries to enter the pool area. Cell-Mounted Infrared Detector pool alarms use motion sensors and infrared technology to detect any movement around the pool. Underwater Motion Alert systems are placed underwater and monitor the pool for any sudden movements, such as a child falling in. These different types of pool alarms provide layers of protection by detecting unauthorized access or potential emergencies in and around the pool area.</p></p>
