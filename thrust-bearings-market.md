<p><p>Thrust bearings are designed to handle axial loads and are divided into two main types: Ball Thrust Bearings and Cylindrical Roller Thrust Bearings. Ball Thrust Bearings utilize balls to support the load and are suitable for applications with lower speed and lighter loads. On the other hand, Cylindrical Roller Thrust Bearings use cylindrical rollers to support heavier loads at higher speeds. Both types of thrust bearings are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and machinery for efficient load handling.</p></p>
